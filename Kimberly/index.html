Information about Coronavirus and Quarantine

What is Coronavirus and how has it affected many people?

Coronavirus (Covid-19) is a disease that started early in the year. Symptoms of this disease include: fever, coughs, shortness of breath, fatigue, muscle and body aches, etc. Other warnings in a person are confusion, trouble breathing, pain and pressure in the chest, inability to stay alert, and changes in the color of the face. Due to the rapid spread of the disease over one million people worldwide have died. Those who have severe medical conditions like heart or lung diseases and diabetes are likely to contract the disease and have serious complications. Those who did not and have not developed Covid-19 in their body have quarantined to avoid getting infected to protect those around them and themselves.

How has quarantine changed the way people live?

Quarantine was imposed when the numbers of cases rised dramatically. Since the comencement of the outbreak the way we live has changed. Schools were closed, forcing students to learn remotely and many adults were left unemployed. Staying at home has caused people to deal with their new lifestyle from home preventing them from associating with others in person. This has affected many emotionally and physically because they do not accept having to be confined within four walls. Due to this many have resorted to eating to forget about the current situation. Quarantine has brought a level of stress upon everyone whether people are not employed and have bills to pay, or students are learning remotely with a huge amount of work.

Healthy Tips to get Fit

  • Plan a routine workout to use for exercise each day in the morning
  • Eat healthy: Do not forget to eat fruits and vegetables
  • Drink plenty of water and fluids with vitamins to boost your immune system
Exercises

It is important to exercise in order to avoid any illnesses and maintain a healthy lifestyle. It is not just about being fit and losing weight, but about gaining energy and boosting your immune system. In addition, daily exercise can help your mental health by feeling better and happier relieving any stress and depression you may have.

Eating Healthy

Along with exercises, eating healthy has a positive impact on your life. Eating fruits and vegetables can reduce the risk of diseases.Vegetables provide nutrients and fibers to your body which can decrease the possibility of developing an infection or a disease.

Importance of Water

Drinking water helps you stay hydrated and avoids dehydration. You can get rid of wastes, keep your normal temperature, and protect your spinal cord and joints. In addition, it boosts your energy and improves blood oxygen circulation which overall is a postive impact on your health.

Meditation

Staying home for a long time and watching news constantly speaking about the deaths increasing can get very overwhelming. Many can start to become depressed or stressed as their situation gets worst by the day. Taking just five minutes of your day to meditate, breath and close your eyes can help your mind clear itself and stop thinking of all the negative happening around. Simply not thinking, can relieve your mind from any stressful thoughts and feel better by getting your mind to relax.

Games

What are some fun entertaining games?

Due to being in quarantine many of us are resorting to using our phones in order to find entertainment. Watching tv, going on social media, and simply sleeping is taking all of our time because instead of accomplishing something productive we are limiting ourselves to spending so much time looking at a screen. One way that this affects us is that by being entertained in theses devices we are losing connection with the people around us. Through this there is no communication or interest to interact socially with others that might be doing the same mistake. Another effect it has on our lives is that we are not putting our minds to work and think rather than just process what we watch. There are many games that help maintains us entertained, occupied, and most of all it forces our brain to function and find ways to win the game!

The following are some games that you may find entertaining:

Jenga

Jenga is a game that requires mental skills and physical strategies. The game consists of stacking blocks in which anyone can play and each person takes a turn to remove a block from the tower and place it on the top. By doing this, the tower will become taller and can start to become unstable causing the structure to collapse. The rules are that one person is to build the tower by placing three bocks next to each other, follow by other three blocks perpendicular to the bottom ones and continuing the process. The players can only use one hand to take one block and place on the top level. The game ends when the tower falls over or if a block falls.

UNO

UNO is one of the classic games that can be enjoed with the whole family. This game is played by players taking turns to match the card before with a card with the same color or number. If one player does not have a card that matches the previous then they have to grab from the center pile until they find a card. In this game there are special cards that make the game more fun and interesting to play such as the Skips, Reverses, Draw Twos, Draw Fours, and the Wild cards. When one player comes down to their last card they need to shout "UNO!" When the person manages to get rid of all the cards they are declared the winner.

Scrabble

Scrabble is a game that can be played by two people. The players create words with the tiles given on the board and each have point values that can rise the value of the tile or the word. The skills needed are to have advance vocabulary, great spelling, and importantly a strategy. All the words formed need to be defined in the dictionary. One letter that has a lot of worth is the letter "Z" and there are many four lettered words that contain the letter "Z" that are considered legal in the Scrabble dictionary. Some words that can be memorized are AZAN, BOZO, CHEZ, FAZE, HAZE, HAZY FUZZ, JAZZ, LAZY, MAZE, OOZE, QUIZ, ZETA, ZONE, ZOOM, SIZE, and COZY.

Pictionary

Pictionary is a fun game that can be played with family members or with friends. This game is a drawing game in which one person let's say from a team of two draws the image that they got based on the card they picked. Then the other person from the team has one minute to guess what the drawing is with a phrase or word. The game provides 4 category cards, 496 cards in total, a dice, 4 pads to draw, 4 pencils and the rules. If the person guesses right then they get to roll the dice that detrmines the next category. They can move forward to drawing, but if there teammate does not guess correctly then the opposing team has the chance to go next. If you don't have the game then you can just draw on a paper and write your own words and phrases. Then you can just make teams and have fun playing it.
